Available for Adoption

Foster homes desperately needed! Please contact us if you would like more information on being a temporary foster home for a pug.

All animals are spayed/neutered and current on all vaccinations prior to adoption. If you are not in the Las Vegas area, please check our Pug Rescue Links page to find a Rescue in your area.

Note: A vet history is required to adopt a rescue pug.

Jonah

Meet Jonah, a big, handsome boy estimated to be 6 to 8 years old. Jonah was surrendered because he was having issues with the bigger dogs in the house. He seems to be fine with other dogs out in public but can get jealous of other dogs in his home and become aggressive, so he will probably need to be an only dog. Jonah loves all people and attention, and other than needing to lose some weight, he is healthy, happy boy.

Soprano

Meet Soprano! He is a beautiful 10 year old black pug, who came into rescue with Bella but they can be adopted separately. He's a fantastic pug. His foster home reports that he gets along with everyone, is very welll behaved, is great on a leash and loves chasing tennis balls. He is also the sweetest boy who ADORES being cuddled and carried...he even gives you hugs when you pick him up. He would fit in with pretty much any type of home.

Remington is ready for his forever home!

I'm Remington (Remi for short), and I have a lot to be thankful for this holiday season. I was hit by a car (that's not the part I'm thankful for), but then I was turned over to SNPR to make sure I get the care I need. The car accident left me very bruised and with road rash all over. I also have a very bad knee, and I heard the vet tell SNPR that it is the same grade as a pug named Bleu's was and that I will need surgery for a luxating patella. But SNPR said "we fixed Bleu up, and we'll get you fixed up too!" and gave me a kiss. I knew I had landed in a good place. I also need a dental, and my vision is limited...however, I'm extremely sweet and good natured. I really like to cuddle, and I get around fine. I'm about 8 years old, and I'm already making my holiday wish list-to find a great family! I will need to go back to the vet in a couple weeks after I am less sore to see when I need my surgeries, so please consider making a donation, because I know it will be expensive and they have lots of pugs to save this holiday season!

Frankie

Jordy

Southern Nevada Pug Rescue is an all-volunteer organization dedicated to the rescue and placement of pugs in need.

Our mission is to rescue neglected, homeless, unwanted, sick or injured pugs, rehabilitate them, and place them in loving, permanent homes in the Southern Nevada area. We strive to educate the public about the pug dog breed, to encourage responsible pet ownership, and to see to it that any pug that comes through our rescue ends up in a caring, committed environment.
