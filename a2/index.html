Want to start off 2013 by saving a life?

Please consider fostering for Southern Nevada Pug Rescue.

We are especially in need of foster homes for the following: males of all ages, pairs, pugs that may need a little more time in rescue due to medical issues or pugs that may need some basic training (some of our pugs have never had the luxury of being an indoor pet so they need some loving guidance).

If you adopted from us, your pug was fostered by a very special home so please consider opening your home to a pug who needs you now. SNPR can only take in pugs if we have foster homes, they are critical to our success! Even if you only foster one time, that helps us make room for one more. If you adopted from us, you are already approved. Just email saira@rescuepugs.com to be added to our list. Or apply online at www.rescuepugs.com, put in an application and mark that you are interested in fostering. Remember SNPR will take care of the medical expenses for the foster pugs, you provide the love.

Southern Nevada Pug Rescue is an all-volunteer organization dedicated to the rescue and placement of pugs in need.

Our mission is to rescue neglected, homeless, unwanted, sick or injured pugs, rehabilitate them, and place them in loving, permanent homes in the Southern Nevada area. We strive to educate the public about the pug dog breed, to encourage responsible pet ownership, and to see to it that any pug that comes through our rescue ends up in a caring, committed environment.
