Vegas Burger Blog
Client

Erik Chudy

Project Info

Custom WordPress theme, logo design

URL

vegasburgerblog.com

Erik Chudy commissioned the Vegas Burger Blog as a sister site to his brother's Boston Burger Blog. Building off the original Boston theme, the Vegas Burger Blog was branded with a color scheme and logo more identifiable with Las Vegas.

Since launching in 2011, the site has grown to record around 2,000 pageviews per week with a peak of over 20,000 views during the recent "2013 VBB Burger Tournament."
